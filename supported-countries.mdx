Karma partners with Bridge and regulated financial institutions. Availability depends on your country of residence and local regulations.

Definitions

- **Prohibited**: Not available.
- **Controlled**: Available only via specially approved programs.
- **Restricted**: Available with additional monitoring and controls.
- **Not High Risk**: Available with standard monitoring and controls.

If you’re unsure about your eligibility, contact support via [Telegram](https://t.me/karmawallet).
