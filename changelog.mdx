## June 11, 2026

### 📣 The Feed: Social Trading Comes to Karma

Karma now has a built-in social feed. Every post is tied to a token, and the author's portfolio value plus the token's price are snapshotted at post time, so every call is accountable.

- **Post your calls**: an X-style composer with image attachments, @mentions, and $cashtags with inline autocomplete. Tagging a $token attaches its live mini chart to the post.
- **For You and Following**: two feed tabs with the native large-title look and pull-to-refresh.
- **Tap into any post**: a full detail view with a live price chart and a buy/sell terminal, so you can act on a call without leaving it.
- **Drafts and lightbox**: drafts save privately on your device and survive restarts, and images open in a pinch-to-zoom lightbox.
- **Profiles show the receipts**: profile pages now have Posts, Positions, and History tabs, so anyone's calls sit right next to their track record.

### ⚖️ Versus Posts

Compare two tokens side by side in a single post. Each side gets its own thesis and image, readers can open either token's full chart, and an optional relative-value mode shows what one token would be worth at a chosen fraction of the other's market cap, with the gap multiple right on the card.

### 🛡️ Safety Built In From Day One

Every post passes automated moderation of both text and images before it publishes. You can report posts, block accounts (blocking hides you from each other entirely), and delete your own posts. Repeatedly reported posts come down automatically, and posting is rate-limited to keep spam out of the feed.

### 🐞 Smaller Fixes

- Brand-new Base tokens now show the correct symbol and amount in your portfolio instead of an unknown placeholder.
- Editing your profile no longer hides input fields behind the keyboard.
- Following a token from its page replaces the old watchlist star with a clear Follow pill.

## June 3, 2026

### 🟦 Base Is Live

Base trading is now generally available inside Karma. You can buy, sell, hold, and swap Base tokens alongside Solana — all from the same accounts and the same flows you already know.

- **Multi-chain swaps**: buy a Base token paying with your Solana balance (or vice versa) in a single transaction.
- **Native Base sells**: sell any Base token you hold back to USDC, EUR, or any supported asset.
- **Gas, handled**: ETH gas for Base trades and sells is sponsored automatically — you don't need to top up ETH first.
- **Pre-flight checks**: if a route can't go through, you get a clear, friendly message up front instead of a failed swap.

### 📈 Base Tokens in Trending, Search & Charts

Trending now ranks tokens across Solana and Base by turnover with a chain filter, so you can scope to one chain or see everything together. Search returns results from both chains in a single unified list. Sparklines, prices, charts, and token metadata are all wired up for Base.

### 🪪 Phantom-Style Chain Badges

Tokens across the app now show a small chain mark on their icon, so Base and Solana assets are instantly distinguishable in lists, the home portfolio, and search results.

### 💼 Unified Portfolio

Your home portfolio now merges Base holdings with Solana, including Base USDC. P&L, snapshots, and totals include both chains. The wallet switcher folds your Base wallet under your primary Solana wallet as a single account with per-chain rows.

### 🔔 Notification Polish

Push notifications for deposits, settlements, and swaps now correctly distinguish between chains and trade types — no more duplicated or mislabeled alerts during multi-step flows.

### ⚡ Smaller Quality-of-Life Wins

- Token detail page hides the Trades tab while not in view for snappier scrolling and lower battery drain.
- Empty-state, shimmer loading, and explore-settings polish across search and trending.
- Card top-up now spans your Solana and Base balances for Max and validity checks.
- On-ramp transaction detail leads with the EUR amount you paid, hiding intermediate placeholders.
