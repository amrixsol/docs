During business onboarding, Karma requires a tax identification number or equivalent business registration number. The accepted identification types vary by country.

<Note>
  In addition to the country-specific types listed below:
  - **`other`** is accepted for **all countries** — provide a description of the document.
  - **`ssn`**, **`itin`**, **`ein`**, and **`passport`** are accepted for **US-based sole proprietorships**.
  - **`passport`** and **`national_id`** are accepted for **non-US-based sole proprietorships**.
</Note>
