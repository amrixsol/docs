Formation documents prove that your business was legally established. The specific documents required depend on your entity type and jurisdiction.

## By entity type

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Sole Proprietorship">
    One of the following:
    - Business license
    - Trade name registration
    - DBA (Doing Business As) filing
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Partnership">
    **General Partnership** — one of:
    - Partnership agreement
    - Business license listing all partners
    - Trade name registration listing partners

    **Limited Partnership (LP)** — Certificate or Registration of Limited Partnership filed with the secretary of state

    **Limited Liability Partnership (LLP)** — Certificate or Registration of Limited Liability Partnership filed with the secretary of state
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Corporation">
    One of the following, filed with the secretary of state:
    - Articles of Incorporation
    - Certificate of Incorporation
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="LLC">
    One of the following, filed with the secretary of state:
    - Articles of Organization
    - Certificate of Organization
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Nonprofit">
    - Articles of Incorporation or Certificate of Incorporation (filed with the secretary of state)
    - Application for Tax-Exempt Status (IRS Form 1023 or 1023-EZ, for US entities)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Foundation">
    Same as Corporation:
    - Articles of Incorporation or Certificate of Incorporation (filed with the secretary of state)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="DAO">
    If the DAO is established as a legal entity (e.g. LLC), standard formation documents apply.

    Otherwise, acceptable alternatives include:
    - White paper or governance framework
    - Membership agreements
    - Governance documentation outlining structure and decision-making
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Trust">
    - Trust Deed
    - Certificate of Trust
    - Notarized Trustee's Affidavit
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Cooperative">
    - Articles of Incorporation
    - Bylaws
    - Membership Agreement
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Regional notes

<Note>
  **Mexico**: A copy of the entity filing document (*Cédula de Identificación Fiscal*) confirming the entity's RFC is required.
</Note>
