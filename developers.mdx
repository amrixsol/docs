We’re preparing a simple, well-documented API with OpenAPI specs and examples. Stay tuned in the docs and our channels for updates.

You’ll be able to integrate Karma’s products directly into your project, including:

- Issuing IBANs
- Issuing US routing and account numbers
- On/off-ramps
- KYC and verification flows

If you’re interested in early access, reach out via [Telegram](https://t.me/karmawallet).
