## Current status

\$KARMA is live on Meteora. Our token is powered by a locked liquidity pool on Meteora.

- Fees per swap: 2% total
  - 0.4% protocol fee to Meteora
  - 1.6% to the Karma treasury (under our agreement with Meteora)
- Treasury split: 50% \$KARMA and 50% SOL
- Purpose: grow protocol-held supply/liquidity and fund operations
 - Circulating impact: 50% of fees accrue in \$KARMA, reducing circulating supply on every trade

Product integration is in progress; no in-app utility is active yet.

## Vision

Align platform performance with token holder upside, responsibly and compliantly.

## Live utility

- **[Staking](/staking)** — stake $KARMA in-app to unlock KP multipliers (up to 3×) and swap-fee discounts (up to zero fees). No APY; benefits are product utility, not yield.
- **Fee accrual** — 50% of every $KARMA swap accrues to the treasury in $KARMA, reducing circulating supply.

## Planned utility (exploration)

- Possible use of a portion of fees for buybacks
- Higher card limits tied to staking tiers
- Governance signaling first, then formalization if appropriate

The above are not live today; details will be announced before launch.

## Not live yet

- No yield-bearing staking, liquidity mining, or rev-share distributions
- No governance portal
- Token not required to use current features (staking is opt-in)
