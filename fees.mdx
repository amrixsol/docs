We charge zero FX margin on bank transfers. What you see is what you get.

## Simple pricing

### Card payments
- **Karma service fee**: 0.5% per transaction
- **FX fee**: 1% on non-USD transactions
- **International transaction fee**: \$0.30 flat network fee charged by Visa on non-USD transactions

### Bank transfers
- **On-ramp fee**: 0.5%
- **Off-ramp fee**: 0.5%
- **SEPA transfer**: \$1.45
- **ACH transfer**: \$0.95
- **FX margin**: None — real-time rates, zero markup

### General
- **No subscriptions**: No signup, monthly, or hidden fees

All fees are shown before you confirm. Rates are fetched live and displayed in app.
