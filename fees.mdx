# Fees

We use the most real‑time FX rates available and charge zero FX margin. What you see is what you get.

## Simple pricing

- **Swap fee**: 0.85% per conversion (same as Phantom)
- **IBAN/Routing withdrawals**: No extra fees from us
- **No subscriptions**: No signup, monthly, or hidden fees

Any network or third‑party banking fees (e.g., your sending bank) are shown before you confirm. Rates are fetched live and displayed in app.
