## Simple pricing

### Card payments
- **Karma service fee**: 0.5% per transaction
- **FX fee**: 1% on non-USD transactions
- **International transaction fee**: \$0.30 flat network fee charged by Visa on non-USD transactions

### Bank transfers
- **On-ramp fee**: 1%\*
- **Off-ramp fee**: 0.5%
- **SEPA transfer**: \$1.45
- **ACH transfer**: \$0.95
- **FX margin**: None — real-time rates, zero markup

### Trading (Solana token swaps)
Karma routes swaps through Jupiter and aggregates the best price across Solana DEXes. We charge a single in-app fee on top — no spread markup, and no separate Jupiter platform fee on your end.

- **Karma swap fee**: 0.5% of trade size
  - **Minimum $0.45** per trade (floor for very small trades)
  - **Maximum 2.55%** (cap on tiny trades — kicks in below ~\$17.65)
  - At \$90+ the floor is reached, so you always pay a flat 0.5%
- **Network gas**: **Sponsored by Karma** on the default route (Jupiter Ultra, gasless). Only the legacy v6 fallback — used in rare edge cases — passes Solana network gas (typically <\$0.01) on to you.
- **DEX/LP fees**: Set by the underlying liquidity venue (Raydium, Meteora, Orca, etc.) and already reflected in the price you see. We do not add a markup on top.

#### Staking discount

Stakers pay less or nothing on the Karma swap fee. The discount applies on top of the base 0.5%:

| Staked $KARMA | Effective swap fee |
| ------------- | ------------------ |
| < 1M          | 0.5% (full fee)    |
| 1M+           | 0.25% (50% off)    |
| 10M+          | 0% (free swaps)    |

See the [Staking](/staking) page for details on how staking tiers work.

### General
- **No subscriptions**: No signup, monthly, or hidden fees

All fees are shown before you confirm. Rates are fetched live and displayed in app.

\*Additional fees may apply for third-party deposits, high-risk transactions, or enhanced due diligence requirements.
