## Is there an APY?

**No.** Staking $KARMA does not pay a percentage yield. We do not run a staking program that mints or emits rewards.

Instead, staking unlocks **product benefits** inside the app: a higher Karma Points (KP) earn rate on every card purchase, and progressively reduced fees. The more you stake, the better your rate stays — your $KARMA keeps working as long as it is staked.

## Why no APY?

Yield-bearing staking would require either token emissions (which dilute holders) or guaranteed payouts from treasury (which create regulatory exposure in many jurisdictions). We chose the simpler, more sustainable path:

- **Stakers get utility, not yield.** Lower fees on swaps, higher KP multipliers on card spend.
- **The treasury keeps its 50% fee accrual** in $KARMA. That mechanic — not emissions — is what aligns long-term holders with the platform.
- **You can unstake at any time** (subject to a 7-day cooldown — see below).

## Tiers and benefits

There are three staking tiers. Each tier is unlocked by reaching a minimum staked amount. Benefits stack with your existing rewards tier (Seed / Rising / Elite / Black), which is determined separately by your KP balance.

| Staked $KARMA | KP multiplier | Fee discount        |
| ------------- | ------------- | ------------------- |
| 0             | 1×            | —                   |
| 100,000       | 1.5×          | —                   |
| 1,000,000     | 2×            | 50% off swap fees   |
| 10,000,000    | 3×            | Zero swap fees      |

### What the multiplier applies to

The multiplier scales **Karma Points earned on card purchases**. KP convert into cashback at your rewards tier:

- **Seed:** 5% cashback baseline
- **Rising:** 10%
- **Elite:** 15%
- **Black:** 25%

So a 2× boost on a Rising-tier user effectively doubles the KP rate on each swipe — accelerating progression to the next rewards tier and increasing the cashback flowing back to you.

### What the fee discount applies to

The fee discount applies to **the Karma swap fee** on Solana token trades inside the app. The base swap fee is 0.5% (with a \$0.45 minimum floor on small trades) — see [Fees → Trading](/fees#trading-solana-token-swaps) for the full breakdown.

- **1M+ staked**: 50% off → effective 0.25% swap fee
- **10M+ staked**: zero swap fee

Network gas is already sponsored by Karma on the default route regardless of staking. Ramp fees (on-ramp / off-ramp) and external partner fees are unaffected by staking.

## How to stake

1. Open the **Profile** tab in the Karma app.
2. Tap your **$KARMA balance** in the staked section, or go to **Stake $KARMA** from the rewards screen.
3. Enter the amount you want to stake and confirm.

Your staked balance shows up immediately under "Staked" on the Profile tab.

## Unstaking

Unstaking has a **7-day cooldown** before tokens are returned to your wallet. You can initiate an unstake at any time from the rewards screen.

- During the cooldown your tokens still count as **staked** for tier purposes — you keep your boosts until the window ends.
- Once the cooldown completes, the tokens unlock and your staking tier recalculates based on your remaining staked balance.
- The cooldown exists to prevent gaming tier benefits with short-term deposits and to keep the staking pool stable.

## What staking is *not*

To set expectations clearly:

- **Not a yield product.** No APY, no emissions, no rev-share distributions to stakers.
- **Not custodial lending.** Your $KARMA is not lent out to third parties.
- **Not a lockup beyond cooldown.** You can exit any time; the 7-day window is the only constraint.
- **Not required.** All Karma features work without staking — staking only enhances them.
